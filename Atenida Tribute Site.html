"Mama" Athenida Recio

Family Above All

Atenida Recio, otherwise known as "Mama Atenida", was a strong family woman known thoughout her community. She raised her four daughters to be strong independent women who each lead their individual families. She is survived by her four daughters, starting with the oldest is Rosemary, Rosabel, Mildred, and Diana; her 11 grandchildren and 1 great grandchild.
