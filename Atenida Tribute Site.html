"Mama" Athenida Recio

Family Above All

Atenida Recio , otherwise known as "Mama Atenida", was a strong family woman known thoughout her community. Atenida was born in Las Guaranas, Dominican Republic on November 14th, 19XX. She moved to New York City to live and raise her daughters in her humble city apartment. Members of the community would remember her for all of the cooking that she did. She was quite the popular cook and her recipes still live on today though her family. She raised her four daughters to be strong independent women who each lead their individual families. She is survived by her four daughters, starting with the oldest is Rosemary, Rosabel, Mildred, and Diana; her 11 grandchildren and 1 great grandchild. She died from Pancreatic Cancer on June 4th, 2018.
